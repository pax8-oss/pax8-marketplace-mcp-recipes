# Example Prompts

Curated example prompts, grouped by use case. Each maps to one or more recipes in `SKILL.md`.

---

## Companies

```text
"List all my active companies."
"Show me the details for Acme Co."
"Do I have any inactive companies?"
"Find companies with 'tech' in the name."
```
